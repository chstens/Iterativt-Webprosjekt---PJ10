The game room - BattleStation NITH - is available to students for both schoolwork and recreation Monday through Thursday.


The game room is available during office hours at the school (9am - 3pm.) Keys to the game room are kept at the library and can only be borrowed with a student card.

Submission takes place in the box by the door in the game room. NB! Do not take the key home.
One key set per person. (One set provides access to a console cabinet.)

Anyone registered as a borrower of a key is held responsible for the contents in the game room. In most cases, this includes: console, controller(s), games, and headset. The cabinet contents are routinely checked.

Loss/Damage of property is set at fixed rates.

Compensation amount:

  • Games: kr 500,-
  • Controllers: kr 500,-
  • Headset: kr 400,-
  • Console: kr 4000,-

The library reserves the right to change the rates of compensation for specific items. Borrowers who fail to comply with lending rules may be denied future loans. On Fridays and weekends, the room is reserved for groups and committees. The room can be booked for special events. Contact the library!
