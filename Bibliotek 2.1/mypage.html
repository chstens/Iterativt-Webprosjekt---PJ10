Bibsys: My page

The Bibsys My page allows you to reserve or renew loans, as well as reserve interlibrary books. Login at the top right of Bibsys Ask. To use this site you must be a registered borrower with a valid e-mail address by a Bibsys library.

The first time you log in you need to order a password which will be promptly sent by e-mail. To create a new password follow these steps:

  1. Select "Request password" in the menu on the login page.
  2. Enter borrower ID and last name. Your borrower ID is NITHxxxxxx where the x's are a student number.
  3. Select the e-mail address from the drop down menu in the next screen. Click "Request password". Unfortunately, there is an error in the system that does not allows the password to be sent to hotmail addresses. If you are registered with a hotmail address, you must contact the library and get a new email address before you receive your password.
  4. Sign in with your username (borrower ID) and password.


You can renew or reserve loans as well as reserve interlibrary lending books.

Attention! Students at NITH can access books freely from the bookshelves. Books can be ordered via MinSide if the books are checked out or if they are not available at NITH. When a book in Bibsys is reserved or borrowed the book, you will be notified by SMS or email when the book can be picked up.
