Check-Out Rules NITH Library

  • The library service is primarily for NITH students and staff. Non-students can access, but extended service funding / training / helpis not provided. During busy use of materials / computer times are reserved for our own borrowers.
  • A loan can be renewed up to 3 times if not reserved by others. Other items available for check-out cannot be renewed. (See more about using Bibsys MyPage)
  • The borrower is responsible for borrowed material and requested to meet the deadline. After 3 notices financial liability at fixed rates is set.
  • Borrowers who fail to comply with lending rules may be denied future loans.
  • Students at NITH can freely access books from shelves in the library. If the books are loaned out, or not in stock at your own library you can submit your reservation. The book will be reserved or borrowed, and you will be notified by SMS or email when it can be retrieved.
  • The guidelines also apply to employees at NITH.

Compensation amounts for loss of books

  • Books: NOK 500, -
  • Games: NOK 500, -
  • Controls: NOK 500, -
  • Headsets: NOK 400, -
  • Console: NOK 4000, -
  • Journals, or very expensive material: Purchase price plus NOK 150, - in charge Borrowed material is replaced by holding the library rates.

The library reserves the right to change the rates of compensation for specific items. Borrowers who fail to comply with lending rules may be denied future loans. Fridays / over the weekend is the space reserved for groups and committees. The room can be booked for special events. Contact the library!
